Stay Safe Online – Checklist (India)
✅ Prevention Tips
Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, banking, and social media
Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or banking details with anyone
Do not scan unknown QR codes or approve “collect” requests blindly (UPI safety)
Verify payment requests before sending money, even if they appear from known contacts (UPI safety)
Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp
Keep your apps and devices updated to protect against security vulnerabilities
Limit personal information shared on social media (location, phone, college details)
Log out from public/shared computers after use
Install apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store
